# Privacy Policy

**SpeedSync (Android Application)**
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-26
**Contact:** support@speedsync.dev

---

## 1. Overview

SpeedSync is a mobile application that allows you to sync your Speediance
workout history to your Garmin Connect account. The application is
designed with a privacy-first, on-device architecture:

- No developer-operated servers
- No cloud storage controlled by the developer
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs

All processing occurs locally on your device and is initiated by you.

---

## 2. Controller and Contact

SpeedSync is operated by an independent developer acting as the person
responsible for personal information under applicable privacy laws.

**Contact:** support@speedsync.dev

---

## 3. Personal Information We Process

We process only the information necessary to provide the application's
functionality:

### 3.1 Authentication Data
- Speediance email and password
- Garmin email and password
- Authentication tokens (session, access, refresh tokens)

These are treated as sensitive authentication data.

### 3.2 Workout Data
- Exercise history (sets, repetitions, weights, timestamps)
- Heart rate samples

This may be considered health-related information. It is processed only
in device memory and is not stored by the application.

### 3.3 Application Data
- Synced workout identifiers
- Last sync timestamp
- Purchase entitlement status
- Free usage counters

---

## 4. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to:

- Authenticate with Speediance and Garmin
- Retrieve workout history
- Convert workouts into Garmin-compatible format
- Upload workouts to Garmin Connect
- Manage paid feature access

We do **not** use your data for:

- analytics
- advertising
- profiling
- marketing

---

## 5. Where Your Data Is Processed

All processing occurs:

- On your device
- Directly between your device and:
  - Speediance
  - Garmin

We do not store or process your data on any developer-controlled server.

---

## 6. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not share your data with third parties under our control.

However, at your request, data is transmitted directly to:

- Speediance
- Garmin

These services act as independent data controllers.

### 6.1 International Users

SpeedSync is available worldwide.

By using the application, you understand that your data may be processed
in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, depending on where
third-party services (Speediance and Garmin) operate.

All transfers occur directly between your device and these services at
your request.

---

## 7. Legal Basis

Processing is based on:

- Your explicit consent and actions when using the application
- Performance of a service you request

You may withdraw consent by stopping use of the application and removing
data from your device.

---

## 8. Storage and Retention

- Credentials and tokens are stored securely on your device using
  encryption
- Workout data is processed in memory only
- Application data is stored locally
- Data is retained until you take action, including:
  - signing out
  - clearing app data
  - uninstalling the app

---

## 9. Security

We implement:

- AES-256 encryption for stored credentials
- Android Keystore for key protection
- HTTPS/TLS for all network communication
- No backups of application data
- Minimal permissions (internet only)

No system is fully secure. You are responsible for securing your device.

---

## 10. Incident Handling

If a security issue occurs:

- We may release updates via Google Play
- Users may be informed via in-app messages or release notes

Where required by applicable law, we will notify users and/or
authorities. Because we do not collect contact information, individual
notification may not always be possible.

---

## 11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- access your data
- correct your data
- request deletion
- withdraw consent

Since all data is stored locally, you can exercise these rights by:

- signing out
- clearing app data
- uninstalling the app

---

## 12. Children

SpeedSync is not intended for children and does not knowingly target
individuals under 13 years of age.

---

## 13. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted in-app
or via the Play Store listing.
